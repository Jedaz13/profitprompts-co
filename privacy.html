Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

ProfitPrompts.co ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website profitprompts.co. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our website and purchase our products.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: When you purchase a product through our Stripe payment links, we collect your email address to deliver your purchase and communicate with you about your order.
  • Payment Information: Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment details on our servers. Please refer to Stripe's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your payment data.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data through Google Tag Manager and Meta Pixel, including pages visited, time spent on pages, and interactions with our website. This data is used to improve our website and advertising.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the digital products you purchase
  • To send order confirmations and product updates
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To run targeted advertising campaigns on platforms like Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
  • To analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following tracking technologies:

  • Google Tag Manager: Used to manage and deploy marketing tags on our website without modifying the code directly.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel): Used to track conversions from Meta ads, build targeted audiences for future ads, and remarket to people who have taken action on our website.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Stripe: For payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Google (Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics): For website analytics and tag management. Google Privacy Policy
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): For advertising and conversion tracking. Meta Privacy Policy

Data Retention

We retain your email address and purchase records for as long as necessary to provide you with our products and services, and as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@profitprompts.co.

Children's Privacy

Our website and products are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
